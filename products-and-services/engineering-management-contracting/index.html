Engineering Management Contracting

We take pride in planning, executing and managing complex projects. With a focus on developing markets, we offer tailor made solutions to meet needs. Along with our experienced partners we provide continued technology & service innovation and integrative solutions which improve operational efficiency and lead to better results.


Design & Engineering
We help to design projects that are conceptually developed with all the details taken into consideration so that a product functions, performs, and is fit for its purpose. Our services includes,
  • Conceptual/ Feasibility Design
  • Environmental consultancy and study
  • Simulation and 3d smart modelling
  • Process safety design
  • Basic and Front engineering design
  • Stress Analysis
  • Detailed Engineering
  • Project Optimization and Troubleshooting

Procurement
All the material and services related to construction are managed by us from end to end project lifecycle such as
  • Logistic Study
  • Material & Spare part management
  • Scouting and management of suppliers
  • Bidding and Bid evaluation
  • Purchasing and contracting activities
  • Negotiating and awarding purchase orders
  • Expediting vendors
  • Coordinating inspection, Logistics and shipping
  • QA / QA document management

Project Management
We use specialised project management technique to plan design and execute a project. We can guide you at every step for your projects in the following manner
  • Scheduling and planning
  • Cost estimating
  • Document control
  • Project supply chain management
  • Project risk management
  • Manpower resources

Construction Management
We make sure that the project's schedule, cost, quality, safety, scope, and function are managed to perfection. During construction of a project, we conduct the following activities
  • Constructability study
  • Field engineering
  • Mechanical completion
  • Commissioning and start up
  • Mark up As-built drawings to mobilization plans and facilities layout
  • Construction supervision and management
  • Construction performance monitoring

Operation and Maintenance
We also provide maintenance and upkeep of projects on a day-to-day basis. Post construction maintenance service includes -
  • Reliability, Availability, maintainability & safety (RAMS) Analysis
  • Reliability Centered Maintained (RCM)
  • Maintained Workload Evaluation and Manning Definition
  • Implementation and optimization of Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS)
  • Programming, Planning and Supervision of Operation and Maintenance Activities (O&M)
  • Asset Integrity Management (AIM) Services
  • Asset Performance Management (APM)
  • Material Management
