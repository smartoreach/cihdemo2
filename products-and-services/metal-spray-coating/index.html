Metal Spray Coating

We provide the highest quality engineered solution for the oil industry for various components like valves, drilling heads, pumps, mud motors, compressor rods, drilling equipment and hydraulics. We make sure to use Tungsten Carbide coatings since it is environmentally friendly and extends the life of critical components too.

Some of our spray coating services are

Diffusion - We offer different kinds of diffusion coating which are Pt-Aluminides, Si-modified Aluminides and Standard aluminides. These coatings can be applied on components of different geometry, dimensions and materials.

Cold Spray - With Cold Spray, we produce high thickness metal coatings with high density and adhesion without residual stresses and oxide content. This technology is extremely suitable for critical dimensional restoration (up to 1 cm on radius).

HIP - Hot Isostatic Pressing (HIP) is a process used to increase the density in meta, and boost their mechanical properties and decrease porosity.

HVAF - High Velocity Air Fuel can be applied as a very thin or thick coat to improve the density and ability to control or define residual stress as the client’s specific application requirements.

HVOF - This coating has better characteristics vis a vis other thermal spray processes. We use a liquid fuel High Pressure HVOF to spray almost oxide free metal coatings and carbides that are water resistant too.

Plasma - Air Plasma spray is the most flexible spray process we offer. This process can be used to melt or plasticize any material from polymers to ceramics. We provide this service for ceramics spraying, abradable coatings and metals with high melting points (like molybdenum).

Shot Peening - We provide shot peening services that are able to perform robot automated shot peening on large components, up to 4m length and 30 ton of weight.

Slurry - We help with slurry coating that is used in many industrial applications. This is the best technique to protect metals from corrosion according to the galvanic principle.

Spray and Fuse - This two step process gives excellent results against aggressive wear problems in corrosion environments such as valve components in severe service. This spray provides a very high bond strength.

Wire Spray - This is a useful process which is relatively inexpensive. We provide this coating service on-site due to the small dimensions of the equipment used.
