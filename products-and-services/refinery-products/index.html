Refinery Products

We supply a variety of Aviation fuels, diesel and gasoline as per European standards and fuel additives as mentioned below.

Aviation Fuel
We supply aviation fuel of two grades which are turbine that is a military grade fuel (Grade F‑34 and F‑44) and Piston engine which is also called AvGas (100 LL and UL91). We supply this fuel in various packing forms. We intend to build a dependable, and consistent supply chain of the fuel.

Diesel EN 590 and Gasoline EN 228
We have a steady supply for diesel too which is a derivation from crude oil. The biggest advantage of diesel fuel is it flows easily in cold temperatures too. Our supply is to power generators and for vehicular use too. We supply gasoline EN 228 as per the European Standards for Gasoline (EN 228) only.

Fuel Additives
These are basically products which are added to petroleum products like diesel and petrol for increasing the efficacy of the fuels. Centane additives for diesel work as a great advantage for the engine performance of the vehicle, proper lubrication of all the parts and minimizing corrosion. In case of Jet fuel additive (FSII - Fuel System Icing Inhibitor), it also works in preventing icing of the fuel. We also supply Marine Fuel Additive works best as a hydrogen sulfide scavenger. There are several kinds of additives which work for enhancing various capacities of the fuel. Our fuel additives can be used for Diesel oil, gasoline, middle distillates, marine and jet fuel.
