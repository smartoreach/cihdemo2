Chemical Supplies

We provide customized chemical solutions in the field of oil and gas, as per the unique needs of the customers. Some of the product categories are mentioned below:

Specialty Chemicals
We produce specialty chemicals as per the unique needs of our customer. We provide our customers with innovative and cost-effective systems to get the specialty chemicals delivered to them. These specialty chemicals have a variety of uses in upstream - for oil and exploration and drilling, midstream - for production, processing and transportation and downstream - for petrochemistry and refineries.

Drilling Additives
Drilling additives are used in the oil and gas industry when oil or natural gas wells are digging for exploration and extraction too. One of the important functions of the additives is to cut out the hole with some ease. We deal in the following additives for oil and gas well drilling; Weighting agents, Viscosifiers, Shale Stabilizers, Filtration control agents, Dispersants, Corrosion inhibitors, Lost Circulations Material, Biocides, Lubricants, Defoams/ Surfactants.

Cementing Additives
We also provide cementing additives for drilling activities. Cementing additives are used to reduce foam in cement systems which is caused during mixing that may lead to pump cavitation and decrease in density. The list of additives supplied by us is accelerators, retarders, gas migration, weighting agents, lightweight/ extenders, dispersants/ friction reducers, defoamers, fluid loss, cement spacer systems and Lost Circulation Materials (LCM).

Blending Solutions
We provide quality control blending solutions that are made in computer controlled, fully automated mixing and weighing systems. We provide the blending vessels for different product solvent types like non polar, organic, polar, water based, foamers, and mixer for small production in IBCs.

Specialty Solvent for Carbon Capture & Storage
The carbon capture and storage or carbon sequestration is considered to be an important solution for long term clearance of CO2 emissions.
