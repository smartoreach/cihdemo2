Mechanical Construction
We can help you in the construction of
  • Mechanical Erection of Chemical Plants, Petrochemical Plants, Gas Plant, Power Plants and other Industrial Plant
  • Design Fabrication and Erection of Steel Structures, Piping System andCryogenic Tanks
  • Pipelines Installation, Piping prefabrication and Electrical and Instrumentation Works
  • EPC Jackets, deck and topside for Off-Shore platform
  • Pre-Assembled Racks (PAR's)and Pre-Assembled Units (PAU's) fabrication and assembly

Operation & Maintenance
Some of the core operations that we undertake are
  • Installation and maintenance of industrial plants in general and in particular oil and gas, petrochemical and power plants, pipelines and pump stations for gas
  • Supply, construction and/or installation of heavy steel structures, platform jackets and decks, and tanks with fixed and floating roofs
  • General prefabrication of piping and relevant pipe supports
  • Complete erection of Turbo gases and relative main and secondary steel structures and ancillaries

Tanks Constructions
For construction and maintenance of tanks for storage of petroleum and other such products, we undertake the following activities:
  • Fixed and floating roof storage tanks for petroleum products
  • Storage and decanting vats for treatment plants
  • LPG tanks, Tanks for drinking water and industrial water
  • Piping and structural steel associated with the storage tanks, and erection in site
  • Prefabrication of the components in the workshop
  • Accessories and instrumentation, tests and inspections, coordination, supervision and final check

Scaffolding & Thermal Insulation
Along with the above the construction work we also take care of the following activities:
  • Installation and maintenance of industrial plants in general and in particular oil and gas, petrochemical and power plants. Surveys, simulations, tests and design for thermal and acoustic insulation
  • Supply and monitoring of preformed insulated coatings, aluminum sheet and pre-insulated systems, for hot, cold and cryogenic works
  • Supply and assembly of rigid polyurethane foam insulated coatings formulated in site, according to the spray and casting technique, and aluminum sheet for hot, cold and cryogenic works
  • Study and Design of noise reduction systems, supply and installation of soundproofing, sound insulation barriers and soundproofed cabins and soundproofing of machinery, equipment, fans, etc.

Civil Works
We also take care of civil engineering jobs like the following:
  • Design, construction, maintenance of civil and industrial works, residential and commercial constructions
  • Construction and maintenance of road works, gas pipelines, oil pipeline, waterworks and sewage systems and purification systems

Offshore & Modules
Offshore construction of the structure and areas surrounding it. Our expertise lies in taking up projects that are managed and constructed by us from start to finish. We also take care of managing these structures for our clients.

Electrical & Instrumentations
We help you with the installation of the electrical and instruments construction too. in the following manner:
  • Electro-instrumentation assembly and installation
  • Test look-check, commissioning and start-up
  • E&I plant systems maintenance
  • For the following applications: Electric distribution; Electric power stations; Iron metallurgy plants; Chemical and petrochemical plants; Pharmaceutical plants; Food plants; Textiles plants; Refineries; Cement factories; Sugar factories; Pipe lines; Gas compression and separation station
