Caspian Investment Holding LLP, located in Almaty, Kazakhstan was founded in the year 2013. The company aims to implement large scale projects in the oil and gas industry, mining industry and trading. The company intends to work with well established global partners to bring international expertise to local needs.

We have partnered with,

  • Rama Petroleum, a global conglomerate of companies for developing the oil and gas industry, mining equipment and trucks.
  • IREM to innovate and develop in the field of construction and mechanical erection works.
  • DG Impianti to manage complex projects and offer tailor made solutions as per the client's needs.
  • Global Chemical Company for customized chemical solutions.
  • Consilliari Partners for trading and supplying crude oil, petroleum products and metals.
  • ItalFluid for specialised solutions in well-test and early production.
  • Alatau Technospray and Flame Spray Spa, for metal spray coating services.
  • Alatau Construction Holding for small scale construction projects.

CIH reinforces partnership with global companies and relationships with clients to build a strong base in fields of oil & gas and mining.
